Featured Project

Simple Pomo Android / Simple Pomo iOS

The Pomodoro Technique is one of my favorite time management strategies, and I just couldn't resist putting my own spin on it with Simple Pomo. Developed as a way to get familiar with Expo and React-Native, Simple Pomo is a clean, minimalist app that provides an ad-free, no-nonsense tool for increasing your productivity.

Built with: Expo, React-Native, Firebase.

Other Projects

Automated Goblin Capitalist

Automated Goblin Capitalist is a single page application designed to simplify the process of earning gold in World of Warcraft using the Inscription trade skill, with long term plans to expand to other trade skills as well. I'm quite fond of finding new and novel uses for APIs, and as far as I know AGC is the only World of Warcraft auction SPA that produces crafting information with the degree of granularity it provides.

Built with: MongoDB, Express, React, and Node.
